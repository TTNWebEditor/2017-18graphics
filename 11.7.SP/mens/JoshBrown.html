Josh Brown

Brown led the American Athletic Conference and ranked 10th in Division I with a 3.5 assist-to-turnover ratio in the 2015-16 season. After Brown started nearly every game at point guard and led Temple in minutes per game, an Achilles injury he suffered in May 2016 limited him to just five games last season. Brown used a medical redshirt to return for his fifth season.
