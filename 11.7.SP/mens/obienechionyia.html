Obi Enechionyia

Enechionyia ranks eighth in program history with 124 blocked shots, and he is 48 points away from 1,000 for his career. Enechionyia finished the 2016-17 season second in scoring and led Temple with 5.8 rebounds per game. He declared for the NBA Draft in March, but he didn't hire an agent, which allowed him to return to Temple for his senior season.
