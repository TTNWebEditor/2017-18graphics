Frank Nutile

With redshirt-sophomore quarterback Logan Marchi dealing with a foot injury, Nutile has started the Owls' last three games under center. The redshirt-junior struggled in relief during Temple's 43-7 loss to South Florida on Sept. 21, when he completed 4-of-12 attempts for 57 yards and threw an interception. In his three starts, Nutile has passed 61-for-89, six touchdowns and two interceptions. He also had a rushing touchdown in Friday's 35-24 win against Cincinnati. Temple has a 2-1 record in games Nutile has started.
