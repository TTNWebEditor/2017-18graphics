Shizz Alston Jr.

With Josh Brown largely unavailable due to an Achilles injury, Alston started every game of the 2016-17 season. He became the first sophomore to lead Temple in points, assists and steals per game since Mark Macon in the 1988-89 season when Macon won the Atlantic 10 Conference Player of the Year Award. Alston scored in double figures in 20 of his final 23 games of the season.
