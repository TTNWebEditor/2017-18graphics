Alliya Butts

Butts will miss the 2017-18 season after suffering an ACL tear on Oct. 11 and having surgery on Oct. 26. She made the American Athletic Conference's second-team list for the second year in a row last season. Butts is the program's all-time leader in made 3-point shots and is seventh on Temple's scoring list with 1,481 points. Butts is expected to return for the 2018-19 season.
